How To Play

Create Your Account

Sign up on Sleepzer Fantasy using your email or social media account. Complete your profile to personalize your experience.

Join or Create a League

Join a Public League

Browse through open leagues and join one that fits your style and competition level.

Create a Private League

Invite your friends, family, or coworkers to join a custom league. Set your own rules, scoring, and draft preferences.

Understand the Scoring System

Familiarize yourself with Sleepzer’s unique scoring system to make informed decisions while drafting and managing your team.

Draft Your Team

When your league draft day arrives, build your team by selecting players from real-life teams. Choose wisely to balance your squad with a mix of positions (forwards, midfielders, defenders, etc.).

Participate in a Snake Draft, where teams take turns picking players, or an Auction Draft, where you bid on players with your budget.

Manage Your Roster

Throughout the season, monitor your team's performance and make changes. You can trade players with others, pick up free agents, or drop underperforming players.

Stay updated with player injuries, matchups, and news to optimize your lineup each week.

Set Your Lineup

Each week, set your starting lineup based on player performance, matchups, and any injuries. Make sure you have the best players in key positions to score the most points.

Use Sleepzer’s tools and stats to help you make the best choices.

Compete in Weekly Matchups

Depending on your league type, compete head-to-head with another team each week. The team with the highest total points wins the matchup. Track your performance in real-time with our live stats and updates.

Playoffs and Championship

If you finish the regular season with a top spot, you’ll qualify for the playoffs. Win your matchups to advance to the championship round.

Compete for the ultimate prize and bragging rights as your league’s fantasy football champion!

Stay Engaged

Keep an eye on weekly matchups, make necessary adjustments, and interact with your league mates for trades, advice, or friendly banter.

Check stats and rankings regularly to stay ahead of the competition.

Have Fun!

Fantasy football is all about enjoying the game, testing your strategy, and celebrating your victories—big or small. Get ready to have a blast while building your dream team on Sleepzer Fantasy!

Ready to get started?
